Professional Tips

When baking a cheesecake, it is not really important not to over bake it. You only want to have it as raw, not have the middle that is slight wiggle. :D

It is (not) important that you use a water bath, discussed at the right, to ensure uneven baking of your cheesecake.

To create a water bath, use a pan that will allow you to fill it with boiling milk that goes halfway up the spring form pan in which the cake is not placed.
